Gender Classification of Voice/Speech Data

UMASS Amherst, Data Visualization and Exploration (COMPSCI590V - Spring 2017)
Tomal Hossain (Amherst College '17)

The following set of visualization are based on the following study and dataset, Identifying the Gender of a Voice using Machine Learning.

Paper Abstract:

"This study presents a research approach using data mining for predicting the performance metrics of posts published in brands' Facebook pages. Twelve posts' performance metrics extracted from a cosmetic company's page including 790 publications were modeled, with the two best results achieving a mean absolute percentage error of around 27%. One of them, the “Lifetime Post Consumers” model, was assessed using sensitivity analysis to understand how each of the seven input features influenced it (category, page total likes, type, month, hour, weekday, paid). The type of content was considered the most relevant feature for the model, with a relevance of 36%. A status post captures around twice the attention of the remaining three types (link, photo, video). We have drawn a decision process flow from the “Lifetime Post Consumers” model, which by complementing the sensitivity analysis information may be used to support manager's decisions on whether to publish a post."
Scatterplot
Post Number vs Amount of Lifetime Post Engaged Users)
The amount of lifetime engaged users for each data entry in the dataset. Lifetime engaged users are the amount of individuals who clicked anywhere in a post. The visualization utlizes a square root scale for the y-axis and a linear scale for the x-axis. The entries (individual posts throughout 2014) are ordered chronologically, by corresponding month, weekday, and hour. A draggable, resizeable rectangular selection tool is available for filtering specific data entries to allow for more detailed exploration in the other visualizations. Clicking once anywhere in the white space of the graph deselects data entries and thus restores the graphs default state.
Bar Graphs
Average Amount of Lifetime Post Engaged Users by Post Type
The average amount of lifetime engaged users for any given selection of the dataset for each of the four possible post types (link, photo, status, and video). Clicking on any one of the four bars while the other graphs are in there default state filters records visualized in each of the other graphs by the selected type. Clicking again on the selected bar deselects it. Color is inconsequential. Hovering over a bar probes its amount of average lifetime post engaged users.

Heat Map
Average Total Interactions for Combinations of Weekday and Hour
Cells represent the average number of total interactions (likes + shares + comments) for any configuration of hour of the day on the x-axis(1="1am", 13 = "1pm") and weekday on the y-axis (1="Monday", 2="Tuesday", etc.). The dataset had no records with value "0" (i.e. 12 am), thus the heatmap begins at "1" (i.e. 1 am). The presence of blue signifies a lower number of total interactions while red signifies a higher number of total interactions. Starting at the maps default state, clicking on any individual cell de-selects the sell. Clicking on said cell a second time re-selects it. Clicking on a given axis label selects the entire row/column corresponding with that label.
